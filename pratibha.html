PRATIBHA BHARADWAJ

LAUNCHPAD’17 CONTESTANT NO.6 - PRATIBHA BHARADWAJ

Pratibha Bharadwaj, a first year student from IIT Delhi is a budding vocalist who has mastered the art of classical Indian music at such a young age. Her choice of music is quite different from the popular culture these days as she rightly believes that the Indian classical music reflects the true shade of the culture of India and probably one of the hardest to learn efficiently. Pratibha is a dynamic personality, participating regularly in athletics and music competitions and shows whenever she gets a chance. Currently she is pursuing graduation in Biochemical Engineering and Biotechnology and has had training in classical Karnataka music since a very young age.

Her parents, particularly her mother, have been very supportive of her talent. “I have been blessed with such supportive parents and they encourage me, and give me strength to face the hardships”, says Pratibha. She adds, “They have never forced me into studies and have made me capable enough to balance my priorities and take decisions wisely”. There have been times when Pratibha felt she would have to quit music or distant herself from it, but with a strong support of her parents, she was able to balance her studies with music.

    • When you are this determined, hardworking and your parents have been equal stakeholders in your struggle, life surely rewards you with a huge reason to smile and pride. This was what happened with Pratibha, as she recalls receiving award from Honorable Governor of Andhra Pradesh for her flawless and mesmerizing performance. It is an achievement that can be a turning point in the life of an individual. Probably the biggest achievement second to being honored by the Governor of the State, as she says, is that she is now a confident performer who knows how to establish contact with her audience.

    • She can read the reactions of the audience and rectify her mistakes to improve her performance. “This is probably the biggest change that music has embedded in my personality. I had to conquer my stage fear if I wanted to perform flawlessly on the stage with all the eyes gazing at me, which I surely wanted to do and be best at it”. Surely her struggle has been fruitful and now, more than ever, she believes that she is on the right path. And yes, she has got a secret ingredient to make your passion a boon for you, and that is honesty and truthfulness to whatever you chose to do. Even God cannot help you if you are not honest in your efforts and dedicated towards your goal. Watch her sing and vote for her here!
