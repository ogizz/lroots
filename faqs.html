FAQs

We at Linking Roots wish to keep everything transparent to our users

1. Should I be already famous to be a part of the artist community?
No. We're not looking for extremely famous artists. Famous or not, you should be extremely talented.
2. I am a writer. Can I apply for joining the community?
We provide the oppotunity to every aspiring writer to work with us as a Blogger! Mail us at linking.roots@gmail.com or send in your query and we'll get back to you.
3. Does Linking Roots have collaborations with other companies?
Definitely. We are in collaboration with many high-profile companies to elevate the opportunities for the artists associated with us.
4. What will I get by joining
Linking Roots?
Exposure, recognition in the field you're passionate about, many opportunities to earn while living the life of your dream and a chance to be inspiration for thousands at once.
5. I have registered but haven't heard back yet. What should I do?
We respond to every query/registration within 15 days. If you still don't hear back, please contact us at 8826992625.
6. I have a query,
how do I ask about it?
You can send us a message on our facebook page or you can please check the Contact Us page.
