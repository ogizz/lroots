GEETIKA MATHUR

LAUNCHPAD’17 CONTESTANT NO.8 - GEETIKA MATHUR

“Geetika Mathur, the girl blessed with a voice so silver-toned. Even her name has “geet” in it. she is currently in her 1st year perusing electrical engineering from IIT Delhi. Her journey with music started from her childhood ,”I have known music since I have known myself” says she. She saw her grandmother and father singing. They were her real gurus, her inspirations.

It was 12th January, an event in her college where she won the title of the best vocalist, has been the highest point of her life till date. She was surprised by the results as she was competing with many renowned singers of her college. Age really doesn’t define your talent and she has outrageously proved it right. It was in class 8th when she went for competing at national level for group singing and they lost. She felt miserably low at that moment but of course she didn’t lose hope, wiped her tears and continued her journey with music being her best friend.

    • “I sing even while studying” says she, that’s what real passion is. Ms. Matrhur , born in a family of musicians shares the best moments with her younger brother who composes songs and she sings it. what an epitome of a sibling relationship! “All I want to say is music requires a lot of hard work and dedication. Believe in yourself and never dependent on anyone” with this, she sings off.
